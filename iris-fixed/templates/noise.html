Health Impacts of Noise Pollution
😴
Sleep Disruption
Noise above 40 dB at night disrupts REM sleep cycles, impairing cognitive function and immune response.
❤️
Cardiovascular Stress
Chronic noise exposure raises blood pressure and increases heart disease risk by up to 20%.
👂
Hearing Loss
Prolonged exposure above 85 dB causes irreversible noise-induced hearing loss (NIHL).
🧠
Cognitive Impairment
Children in noisy environments show impaired reading, memory, and attention span.
